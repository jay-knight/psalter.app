92
1 For *the day before the Sabbath, when the land was first inhabited, the praise of a Song by David.
The Lord reigns; he has clothed himself with honour: the Lord has clothed and girded himself with strength; for he has established the world, which shall not be moved. 2 Thy throne is prepared of old: thou art from everlasting. 3 The rivers have lifted up, O Lord, the rivers have lifted up their voices,† 4 at ‡the voices of many waters: the billows of the sea are wonderful: the Lord is wonderful in high places. 5 Thy testimonies are made very sure: holiness becomes thine house, O Lord, §for ever.
* 92:1 Alex. the sabbath-day. † 92:3 Alex. + the floods will lift up their waves. See Heb. ‡ 92:4 q. d. in answer to. § 92:5 Gr. to length of days.
