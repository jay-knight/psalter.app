69
1 For the end, by David for a remembrance, that the Lord may save me.
2 Draw nigh, O God, to my help. 3 Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek my soul: let them be turned backward and put to shame, that wish me evil. 4 Let them that say to me, Aha, aha, be turned back and put to shame immediately. 5 Let all that seek thee exult and be glad in thee: and let those that love thy salvation say continually, Let God be magnified. 6 But I am poor and needy; O God, help me: thou art my helper and deliverer, O Lord, delay not.
