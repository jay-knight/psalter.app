115
1 Alleluia.
✡ I believed, wherefore I have spoken: but I was greatly afflicted. 2 And I said in mine amazement, Every man is a liar.
3 What shall I render to the Lord for all the things wherein he has rewarded me? 4 I will take the cup of salvation, and call upon the name of the Lord. 4a I will pay my vows to the Lord, in the presence of all his people.
6 Precious in the sight of the Lord is the death of his saints. 7 O Lord, I am thy servant; I am thy servant, and the son of thine handmaid: thou hast burst my bonds asunder. 8 I will offer to thee the sacrifice of praise, and will call upon the name of the Lord. 9 I will pay my vows unto the Lord, in the presence of all his people, 10 in the courts of the Lord's house, in the midst of thee, Jerusalem.
✡ 115:1 2 Cor. 4. 13.
