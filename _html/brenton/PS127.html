127
1 A Song of Degrees.
Blessed are all they that fear the Lord; who walk in his ways. 2 Thou shalt eat the *labours of thy hands: blessed art thou, and it shall be well with thee. 3 Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine on the sides of thy house: thy children as young olive-plants round about thy table.
4 Behold, thus shall the man be blessed that fears the Lord. 5 May the Lord bless thee out of Sion; and mayest thou see the prosperity of Jerusalem all the days of thy life. 6 And mayest thou see thy children's children. Peace be upon Israel.
* 127:2 Alex. fruits of thy labours.
